Homemade Mac and Cheese

Description

This is a nice and rich homemade mac and cheese dish. Serve with a salad for a great meatless dinner.

  • 8 ounces uncooked elbow macaroni
  • 2 cups shredded sharp Cheddar cheese
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cups milk
  • 2 ¼ cup butter
  • 2 ½ tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup bread crumbs
  • 1 pinch paprika
